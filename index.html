About us

Events made easy.

Everest aims to bring a whole new meaning to in-event communication.

From networking events to weddings, our app will bridge the gap between the hosts and guests of an event by vastly improving social interactions among everyone present, as well as increasing organization as a whole.

How it Works

Step 1.

Create an event for your organization through the app.

Step 2.

Guests and other hosts can scan your provided QR codes to enter the event.

Step 3.

Guests and hosts interact between themselves and one-another using communication features.

Features

QR Codes

Allow guests to join your events effortlessly through scanning your event's Guest QR Code.

Event Feed

Provide real-time, salient updates to all guests and hosts through the in-app event feed.

Instant Messaging

Create group chats between hosts and guests to communicate with one another during the event.

Unique Profiles

Create a unique profile per event to showcase a bit about yourself in the event's context. View profiles of other hosts and guests of your event.

List of Guests and Hosts

Gain a high-level understanding of who is attending your event, and keep optimal organization by having a quick reference to all other hosts.
